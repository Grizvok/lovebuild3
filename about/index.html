About

Amy Chiea Clark

Salon Owner/Senior Stylist

Graduated from PBA in 2002. Amy has nearly 20 years experience in the salon industry. She specializes in precision cutting techniques, foil color techniques, keratin smoothing treatments, and formulating Keune Color. Amy’s eye for color is unmatched and second to none. It is Amy’s goal to operate a Salon where everyone feels welcome and relaxed. Amy has a big love for dogs and volunteers as a dog walker for Animal Friends as much as she can. She’s also been a contributor to many organizations such as Mended Hearts of Western PA, Nico’s Warriors, and Pink The Coop to name just a few.

Stevi Richter

Stylist

Stevi is 22 years old and a graduate of Connellsville Area School District, class of 2014. She furthered her education after high school and attended LBI for cosmetology and graduated in September, 2017. She specializes in all types of coloring techniques but balayage is her strength. She loves what she does and strives to make a difference in people’s everyday lives. Stevi has volunteered with Special Olympics Track and Field and The Salvation Army.
